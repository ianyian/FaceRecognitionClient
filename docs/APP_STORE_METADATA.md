**STREAMLINE YOUR SCHOOL ATTENDANCE WITH AI-POWERED FACE RECOGNITION**

FaceAttend revolutionizes school attendance tracking with secure, contactless face recognition technology. Designed specifically for schools and educational institutions, our app makes student check-in fast, accurate, and parent-friendly.

**KEY FEATURES:**

‚úÖ **Instant Face Recognition**
Capture student attendance in seconds using advanced AI face matching. No more manual roll calls or time-consuming sign-in sheets.

‚úÖ **Automated Parent Notifications**
Parents receive instant WhatsApp messages when their child checks in or out, keeping them informed and engaged with school activities.

‚úÖ **Real-Time Dashboard**
Staff can view attendance status, check-in times, and student records at a glance with our intuitive interface.

‚úÖ **Secure & Private**
All face data is encrypted and stored securely in the cloud with enterprise-grade Firebase security. We comply with data protection regulations and prioritize student privacy.

‚úÖ **Multi-School Support**
Perfect for school districts and organizations managing multiple locations. Each school maintains its own secure database.

‚úÖ **Offline Capability**
Continue taking attendance even without internet connection. Data syncs automatically when connection is restored.

**PERFECT FOR:**
- Elementary and Primary Schools
- Secondary Schools and High Schools
- After-School Programs
- Tutoring Centers
- Summer Camps
- Educational Institutions

**HOW IT WORKS:**
1. Staff logs in with secure credentials
2. Student looks at the camera
3. Face is matched against school database
4. Attendance is recorded instantly
5. Parents receive notification via WhatsApp

**PRIVACY & SECURITY:**
- Parental consent required for all students
- Face data encrypted at rest and in transit
- Compliant with COPPA and GDPR regulations
- Staff-only access with multi-factor authentication
- Audit logs for all access and changes
- Data retention policies configurable by school

**REQUIREMENTS:**
- iOS 16.0 or later

- School administrator approval
