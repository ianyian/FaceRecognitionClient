# Privacy Policy for Face Attendance

**Last Updated: December 7, 2025**

## Introduction

Face Attendance ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for attendance tracking in educational institutions.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Used for account authentication and communication
- **Name**: First and last name for staff identification
- **User ID**: Unique identifier assigned to your account
- **Role Information**: Your role (admin, teacher, or reception staff)
- **School/Organization ID**: The tuition center you are associated with

### Facial Recognition Data
- **Face Landmarks**: Mathematical representations of facial features used solely for attendance matching
- **Face Data**: Processed locally on your device using MediaPipe technology
- **Recognition Results**: Attendance records linking recognized students to timestamps

### Usage Data
- **Attendance Records**: Date, time, and location of attendance entries
- **App Activity**: Login times, last active sessions
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version (for compatibility and troubleshooting)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

1. **Attendance Tracking**: Recording and managing student attendance
2. **Authentication**: Verifying staff identity and access permissions
3. **App Functionality**: Enabling core features like face recognition and reporting
4. **Security**: Protecting against unauthorized access
5. **Service Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to improve app performance
6. **Support**: Providing technical assistance when needed

## Face Recognition Technology

### Local Processing
- Face detection and landmark extraction occur **locally on your device**
- Raw camera images are NOT stored or transmitted
- Only mathematical facial landmarks are stored in encrypted form

### Purpose Limitation
- Facial recognition is used **exclusively for attendance purposes**
- No facial data is used for marketing, advertising, or third-party services
- Data is not shared with any entity outside your organization

### Accuracy & Consent
- Face recognition requires initial enrollment with consent
- Students/parents are informed about the system's use
- Alternative attendance methods are available upon request

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location
- Data is stored securely in **Firebase Firestore** (Google Cloud Platform)
- Servers located in secure, industry-standard data centers
- All data transmission uses encryption (TLS/SSL)

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for data in transit
- Encrypted storage at rest
- Role-based access control (only authorized staff can access data)
- Regular security audits and updates
- Firebase Authentication for secure login

### Access Control
- **Admins**: Full access to all school data
- **Reception Staff**: Can view, create, and edit student records
- **Teachers**: View-only access to student information
- No unauthorized third-party access

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share facial recognition data with advertisers
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to attendance
- Provide data to third parties for their marketing

### We MAY share information:
- **With Your School/Organization**: Data belongs to your institution
- **Service Providers**: Firebase (Google) processes data on our behalf per their privacy policy
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or legal process
- **Safety & Security**: To protect rights, property, or safety of users

## Data Retention

- **Active Students**: Face data retained while student is enrolled
- **Inactive Students**: Data archived or deleted per school policy (typically 1-2 years after graduation)
- **Staff Accounts**: Retained while employed; deleted within 90 days of termination
- **Attendance Records**: Retained per institutional requirements (typically 3-7 years)
- **Upon Request**: You can request data deletion (subject to legal/institutional requirements)
