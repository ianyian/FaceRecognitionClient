About Face Attendance

Face Attendance brings cutting-edge facial recognition technology to tuition centers, schools, and training programs. Say goodbye to manual roll calls and time-consuming attendance sheets.

Our application uses advanced MediaPipe AI technology to provide instant, accurate student identification while maintaining the highest standards of privacy and security. All facial processing happens locally on your device, and we never store actual photographs.

Trusted by educational institutions worldwide, Face Attendance helps save hours of administrative time while improving accuracy and providing a modern, professional solution for attendance tracking.

How It Works

  1. Staff login with secure credentials
  2. Select your tuition center from the list
  3. Point camera at student's face
  4. Instant recognition and attendance logged automatically
  5. View and manage attendance records in real-time

System Requirements

  • iPhone or iPad with camera
  • iOS 15.0 or later
  • Internet connection for initial setup
  • Staff credentials from your administrator
