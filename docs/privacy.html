Privacy Policy

Face Attendance Application

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Face Attendance is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you use Face Attendance, we collect:

  • Email address - For authentication and account management
  • Name - To identify staff members within the system
  • School/Institution affiliation - To manage access control
  • Role information - To determine permission levels (Admin, Reception, Teacher)

1.2 Face Recognition Data

For attendance tracking purposes, we process:

  • Facial landmarks - Mathematical representations of facial features (NOT photographs)
  • Face embeddings - Encrypted numerical vectors used for matching
  • Processing metadata - Timestamps and confidence scores
Important: We do NOT store actual photographs of faces. Our system uses MediaPipe technology to process facial features locally on your device and stores only mathematical landmarks.

1.3 Usage Data

  • Attendance records (date, time, student identification)
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device information (model, OS version) for troubleshooting
  • Error logs and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Attendance Tracking - Primary purpose of identifying students and recording attendance
  • Authentication - Verifying staff identity and managing access
  • Service Delivery - Providing core app functionality and features
  • Security - Preventing unauthorized access and detecting fraud
  • Improvement - Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Support - Responding to inquiries and troubleshooting issues
  • Compliance - Meeting legal and regulatory requirements

3. Face Recognition Technology

3.1 Local Processing

Face recognition processing occurs locally on your device using MediaPipe technology. This means:

  • Facial analysis happens on your iPhone/iPad, not on remote servers
  • No images are transmitted over the internet during recognition
  • Enhanced privacy through on-device processing
  • Faster recognition with reduced network dependency

3.2 Data Storage

Only the following data is stored in our cloud database:

  • Mathematical facial landmarks (numerical coordinates)
  • Face embeddings (encrypted vectors for matching)
  • Student identification information
  • Attendance timestamps and records

3.3 Purpose Limitation

Face recognition data is used exclusively for:

  • Student identification during check-in
  • Attendance record verification
  • Improving recognition accuracy within your institution

We do NOT use face data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond attendance tracking.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Cloud Storage

Your data is stored using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with:

  • Encryption at rest - All data encrypted in databases
  • Encryption in transit - HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
  • Access controls - Role-based permissions and authentication
  • Geographic redundancy - Data backed up across multiple regions

4.2 Security Measures

  • Firebase Authentication for secure user management
  • App Check to prevent unauthorized API access
  • Firestore security rules limiting data access by role
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Monitoring for suspicious activities

4.3 Data Retention

  • Active students - Data retained while enrolled
  • Inactive students - Archived after 90 days of inactivity
  • Attendance records - Retained for 3 years for institutional records
  • Face data - Deleted within 30 days upon request or account deletion
  • Staff accounts - Deleted within 90 days of termination

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

5.1 No Sale of Data

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Service Providers

We share limited data with trusted service providers:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud - Cloud hosting and authentication
    • Data processing agreement in place
    • GDPR-compliant data handling
    • Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • MediaPipe (Google) - On-device face detection library
    • Processes data locally (no data sent to Google)
    • Open-source technology
    • Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5.3 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose information when required by law:

  • Court orders or legal processes
  • Government investigations
  • Protection of rights, property, or safety
  • Fraud prevention and security incidents

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our system
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
