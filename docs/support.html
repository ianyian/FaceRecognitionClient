Getting Started

1. First Time Login

  1. Open the Face Attendance app on your device
  2. Enter your email address and password provided by your administrator
  3. Select your school/tuition center from the dropdown
  4. Tap "Login" to access the app
üí° Tip: If you don't have login credentials, contact your institution's administrator. Face Attendance requires authorized staff accounts.

2. Recording Attendance

  1. After login, you'll see the camera view
  2. Position the student's face within the camera frame
  3. Ensure good lighting and ask the student to look at the camera
  4. The app will automatically recognize the student
  5. You'll see a green checkmark and the student's name when attendance is recorded

3. Switching Schools

If you work at multiple locations:

  1. Tap the Settings icon (usually top-right)
  2. Select "Switch School" or "Change Location"
  3. Choose the new school from the list
  4. Continue recording attendance

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if face recognition doesn't work?

A: Try the following:

  • Ensure there's adequate lighting (avoid backlighting)
  • Ask the student to remove glasses, masks, or hats if possible
  • Make sure the student is looking directly at the camera
  • Check that camera permissions are enabled: Settings > Face Attendance > Camera
  • Restart the app if issues persist
Q: Can I use this app offline?

A: Yes! Face Attendance has offline capability:

  • You can record attendance without internet connection
  • Attendance data is stored locally on your device
  • When internet is restored, data syncs automatically to the cloud
  • A sync indicator will show upload status
Q: How secure is the face data?

A: Very secure:

  • All face processing happens locally on your device
  • Only encrypted mathematical landmarks are stored (not photos)
  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Firebase security rules prevent unauthorized access
  • We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Who can access the system?

A: Only authorized staff with credentials:

  • Admin - Full system access, can manage users and view all data
  • Reception - Can record attendance and manage student information
  • Teacher - View-only access to attendance records

Your administrator assigns roles based on your responsibilities.

Q: What if a student isn't recognized?

A: If recognition fails:

  • The student may need to be enrolled in the system first
  • Contact your administrator to add the student's face data
  • Temporary solution: Manual attendance entry (if available in your role)
  • Check if the student's profile is active and properly set up
Q: How do I view attendance history?

A: Depending on your role:

  • Tap on Reports or History in the menu
  • Select date range and filters
  • View individual student attendance or class summaries
  • Export reports if you have admin privileges
Q: What are the system requirements?

A: To use Face Attendance:

  • Device: iPhone or iPad with camera
  • iOS Version: iOS 15.0 or later
  • Camera: Front or rear camera access required
  • Internet: Optional for initial setup; app works offline
  • Storage: At least 100MB free space

Troubleshooting

Login Issues

Problem: Can't log in or getting "Invalid credentials" error

Solutions:

  • Double-check your email and password (case-sensitive)
  • Ensure you've selected the correct school
  • Check your internet connection
  • Contact your administrator to verify account status
  • Try resetting your password (if option available)

Camera Not Working

Problem: Black screen or camera won't activate

Solutions:

  1. Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera
  2. Find "Face Attendance" and toggle it ON
  3. Restart the app
  4. If still not working, restart your device
  5. Check if other apps can use the camera (camera hardware issue?)

Slow Performance

Problem: App is slow or laggy

Solutions:

  • Close other apps running in the background
  • Restart the Face Attendance app
  • Check available device storage (need at least 100MB free)
  • Update to the latest app version
  • Restart your device

Data Not Syncing

Problem: Attendance records not uploading to cloud

Solutions:

  • Check your internet connection (WiFi or cellular data)
  • Look for a sync indicator in the app
  • Wait a few minutes - sync happens automatically
  • If urgent, tap "Force Sync" in Settings (if available)
  • Restart the app to trigger sync

App Crashes

Problem: App closes unexpectedly

Solutions:

  • Update to the latest version in App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Check for iOS updates (Settings > General > Software Update)
  • Uninstall and reinstall the app (data will sync back after login)
  • Contact support with details about when it crashes
